# Feed & Event Imagery

Conventions from `README.md` apply. These power the discovery feed: category tiles that
sit behind event cards and an empty-state illustration. They must feel like *real things
happening near you* — warm, social, never staged dating. No precise/mappable locations;
faces non-identifiable.

> **Tile usage note:** category tiles sit *behind* live UI (event title, time, attendee
> count, fuzzed neighborhood label rendered by the app). Keep the lower third visually
> calm — a soft gradient scrim — so overlaid white text stays legible. No baked-in text.

---

## Event category tiles (each 1536×1024, opaque)

### 1. Cycling & Coast Rides

> Horizontal event-category tile, 1536×1024, opaque. A small crew of friends (18–32,
> diverse) cycling together along a sunlit Bosphorus-side path at golden hour, shot 35mm
> documentary, motion blur in the wheels and background, candid laughter, faces turned or
> motion-blurred and **non-identifiable**. Warm grade, light grain, shallow depth.
> Lower third fades into a soft **Dusk Depth**-tinted scrim for legible overlaid text.
> Mood: free, energetic, social. AVOID: lycra-racing-team corporate look, lone cyclist,
> map/GPS overlays, recognizable landmark or address, romance framing.

### 2. Bouldering & Climbing

> Horizontal tile, 1536×1024, opaque. Three friends at an indoor bouldering gym, warm
> tungsten + window light, one mid-reach on a colorful wall, others spotting and cheering
> below — chalk dust in the air, genuine encouragement. Candid, in-motion, faces side-on
> or below-frame, non-identifiable. Vibrant holds echo coral `#FF5A3C` and mint `#2DD4A7`
> accents naturally. Lower-third soft dark scrim. Mood: supportive crew energy, adrenaline,
> trust. AVOID: solo hero-climber stock shot, extreme-sport ad gloss, gym-brand logos,
> staged smiles at camera.

### 3. Coffee & Hangouts

> Horizontal tile, 1536×1024, opaque. A cozy Kadıköy third-wave café table from above-ish
> 3/4 angle: four hands clinking flat-whites and reaching for pastries, plants and warm
> wood, soft window light, steam rising, an open board-game box edge in frame. Candid,
> warm, no identifiable faces (hands and torsos only is ideal). Editorial food-and-people
> grade, light grain. Lower-third gentle scrim. Mood: easy belonging, conversation.
> AVOID: two-people-on-a-date across a candlelit table, romantic single rose, corporate
> meeting vibe, brand logos.

### 4. Board-Game & Game Nights

> Horizontal tile, 1536×1024, opaque. A warm evening indoor scene: a crew of five around a
> table lit by soft warm lamps, mid-game — laughing, someone rolling dice, cards fanned,
> snacks and a couple of drinks. **Dusk Depth** ambient mood with coral lamp glow. Candid,
> faces turned/blurred, non-identifiable. 35mm, grain, shallow depth. Mood: playful, cozy,
> tight-knit crew. AVOID: poker-gambling-casino vibe, dating-night-in framing, dark/moody
> to the point of unsafe, recognizable faces.

### 5. Markets, Food & Picnics

> Horizontal tile, 1536×1024, opaque. A crew unpacking a colorful picnic on a grassy Moda
> seafront slope at golden hour, simit and seasonal fruit, a blanket, ferries soft-bokeh
> on the water (no mappable landmark). Candid, in-motion, sharing food, faces non-
> identifiable. Warm optimistic grade, grain, shallow depth. Lower-third scrim. Mood:
> generous, communal, sunny. AVOID: romantic two-person picnic, staged influencer flatlay,
> precise location cues, address/signage.

## Empty-state illustration — "No events near you yet" (1024×1024, transparent)

> Square empty-state illustration, 1024×1024, **transparent** background. Semi-flat
> illustration with depth: a friendly abstracted crew of three faceless characters (simple
> rounded forms, inclusive, no facial features) gathered around a softly glowing map-less
> compass / "let's start something" spark, with floating activity glyphs (bike, coffee,
> dice, boulder hold) orbiting on subtle long shadows. Warm gradient fills (Crew Glow
> coral-to-amber on accents), gentle grain, a few hand-drawn imperfect lines for warmth.
> Inviting, optimistic, gently nudging the user to host or widen their search — never sad
> or lonely. No text. AVOID: a single dejected person, empty box/ghost-town sadness,
> literal map with pins, dating imagery, flat corporate-memphis blandness.
