# Marketing — Landing Hero, How-It-Works, Safety

Conventions from `README.md` apply. These sell Tayfa on the web: the first scroll must
make a newcomer feel *"I could belong here, and it's safe."* Premium, alive, trustworthy,
social. Faces non-identifiable; no precise/mappable locations or real brand signage.

---

## 1. Landing-page hero (1536×1024, opaque)

> Wide landing-page hero, 1536×1024, opaque. Cinematic but warm: a diverse crew of five
> friends (18–32) walking-and-laughing together toward a Kadıköy seafront at golden hour,
> one with a bike, one with a board-game box, candid mid-motion — clearly *a crew that
> just met up to do something*. 35mm documentary, warm true-skin grade, light film grain,
> shallow depth, Bosphorus light and soft-bokeh ferries (no mappable landmark). Faces
> turned/side-on/motion-blurred — **non-identifiable**. Composition leaves clean negative
> space on the left over a soft **Crew Glow**-tinted scrim for an overlaid headline in a
> bold geometric-humanist grotesque plus a coral `#FF5A3C` pill CTA. To the right, one
> floating frosted-glass app-UI card (rounded 24px, warm double shadow) previewing an
> event card with a Mint `#2DD4A7` verified tick — Apple-keynote product presentation
> feel. Mood: optimistic, premium, belonging. AVOID: dating/couple framing, hearts/swipes,
> corporate stock, cold blue, recognizable faces or addresses, cluttered text.

## 2. How-it-works triptych (1536×1024, opaque)

> Wide three-panel "how it works" illustration band, 1536×1024, opaque, on a warm Soft
> Cream `#FFF7F0` background with gentle grain. Three connected semi-flat illustrated
> scenes with soft depth, linked by a subtle coral dotted path:
> (1) **Verify** — a frosted profile card with a glowing Mint Verify tick;
> (2) **Discover** — a stack of event tiles with activity glyphs (bike, coffee, boulder)
> and a fuzzed neighborhood blob instead of a precise pin;
> (3) **Meet up** — three abstracted faceless friends high-fiving / clinking cups.
> Consistent friendly geometric icon language, Crew Glow accents, warm-tinted long
> shadows, a few hand-drawn imperfect lines. Inclusive abstracted characters (no faces).
> Mood: clear, joyful, trustworthy. Leave headroom above each panel for a short caption.
> AVOID: literal map with exact pins, flat corporate-memphis, dating imagery, gray shadows,
> baked-in paragraph text.

## 3. Safety section imagery (1536×1024, opaque)

> Wide safety-section visual, 1536×1024, opaque, calm and reassuring. Centerpiece: a large
> frosted-glass panel floating over a softly blurred warm daylight scene of friends meeting
> in public, with a constellation of small trust cues — a glowing **Mint Verify `#2DD4A7`**
> shield-tick, an abstracted "report/block" control rendered as a friendly rounded button
> (never scary), a "women-only / verified-only" filter chip, and a fuzzed-location blob
> showing privacy-by-design. The **Mint Assure** gradient (`#2DD4A7 → #5B8DEF`) appears
> only as a gentle ambient glow. Warm Soft Cream ground, soft warm-tinted shadows, premium
> Apple-grade depth. Mood: protective, transparent, premium — "safety is never an upsell."
> Inclusive, no identifiable faces. AVOID: surveillance/CCTV/creepy-tracking imagery, red
> alarm tones, police aesthetic, precise GPS map, padlock-cliché overload, paywall framing
> around safety.
