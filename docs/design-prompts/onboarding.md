# Onboarding & Taste-Card Heroes

Conventions from `README.md` apply. Onboarding must convert a lonely newcomer into a
hopeful user in seconds: *"there's a crew here doing the things I love, and it's safe."*
Warm, alive, premium. Faces non-identifiable; locations evocative-not-precise.

---

## 1. Welcome hero — "Find your tayfa" (1024×1536, opaque)

> Vertical mobile onboarding hero, 1024×1536, opaque. Editorial 35mm documentary photo
> feel blended with soft UI. A diverse crew of four friends (18–32, mixed genders and
> ethnicities) on a sunny Kadıköy seafront promenade at golden hour, **mid-laugh and
> in motion** — one balancing a bike, one holding two coffees, all leaning toward each
> other candidly. Faces turned, side-profile, or motion-blurred — **non-identifiable**.
> Bosphorus light, ferries soft-bokeh in the background (no mappable landmark). Warm
> golden-hour grade, true warm skin tones, light film grain, shallow depth of field.
> Lower third dissolves into a frosted **Soft Cream `#FFF7F0`** glass panel with a soft
> warm-tinted shadow, leaving clean space for a headline in a geometric-humanist
> grotesque and a **Crew Glow** (`#FF5A3C → #FFB23E`) pill CTA. Mood: optimistic,
> welcoming, belonging. AVOID: posed corporate smiles at camera, romance/couple framing,
> stock vibe, hearts/swipes, cold blue, recognizable faces or addresses.

## 2. Verification & safety step — "Real people only" (1024×1536, opaque)

> Vertical onboarding illustration-photo hybrid, 1024×1536, opaque, reassuring and calm.
> Foreground: a single floating frosted-glass profile card (rounded 24px, soft double
> shadow with warm tint) showing an abstracted faceless avatar and a glowing **Mint
> Verify `#2DD4A7`** verified tick badge with a gentle mint halo. Behind it, a soft
> blurred warm scene of friends meeting in daylight. The **Mint Assure** gradient
> (`#2DD4A7 → #5B8DEF`) appears only as a subtle ambient glow around the trust badge.
> Background is warm Soft Cream with gentle grain. Mood: protective, premium, calm — the
> feeling of "you're safe here," never surveillance or alarm. Semi-flat illustrated
> elements over photographic depth. AVOID: police/shield-as-threat aesthetic, padlock
> clichés overdone, red alarm colors, fingerprint-scanner creepiness, real faces.

## 3. Taste-card stack hero — "Pick what you love" (1024×1536, opaque)

> Vertical hero, 1024×1536, opaque, for the interest/taste-selection screen. A playful
> fanned stack of rounded-corner **taste cards** floating in soft 3D, each card a small
> frosted-glass tile with a single warm-gradient activity illustration: a bike wheel, a
> climbing/boulder hold, a steaming third-wave coffee cup, board-game dice, a ferry, a
> picnic basket. Cards tilt and overlap with depth, parallax, soft warm-tinted shadows;
> the front card is gently lifted and glows with a **Crew Glow** edge. Background: smooth
> Soft Cream-to-coral-tint gradient with grain. Friendly geometric icon style, one
> coherent duotone set (coral `#FF5A3C` + plum `#1E1530`), each with a subtle long shadow.
> Mood: delightful, tactile, optimistic — like choosing your crew's vibe. AVOID: flat
> clip-art, mismatched icon styles, dating/heart imagery, garbled text on cards (keep
> cards text-free, icon-only), gray shadows.

## 4. Single taste-card tile template — "Activity glyph" (1024×1024, transparent)

> A single square taste-card tile, 1024×1024, **transparent** background. One frosted
> Soft Cream glass tile, 28px rounded corners, soft warm double shadow, containing a
> centered duotone activity glyph (coral `#FF5A3C` line + plum `#1E1530` fill) with a
> subtle long shadow and a faint inner top highlight. Friendly geometric icon language,
> premium, alive. Reusable across activities. No text. AVOID: flat sticker look, harsh
> outline, neon, hearts.
