# Shareable Post-Meetup Recap Card

Conventions from `README.md` apply. After a meetup, members can generate a **recap card**
to share to Stories — Tayfa's organic growth loop. It must feel like a premium Spotify-
Wrapped / BeReal-grade keepsake while being **privacy-safe by construction**.

## Hard privacy rules (non-negotiable)

- **No precise location.** Only a fuzzed neighborhood vibe word ("Kadıköy", "by the coast")
  — never an address, venue name, map, or GPS pin.
- **No PII / real faces.** No real recognizable people, no full names, no handles, no exact
  attendee identities. Use abstracted avatars or non-identifiable silhouettes.
- The card is a **template**: the app composites real (already-fuzzed, consented) data over
  the generated art at render time. **Generated art carries no real text data** — leave
  clean zones for app-rendered type. Keep any sample words generic/placeholder.

## 1. Recap card — vertical Stories format (1024×1536, opaque)

> Vertical 9:16-ish shareable recap card, 1024×1536, opaque, premium keepsake feel. Rich
> **Dusk Depth** background (`#3A2A55 → #1E1530`, 160°) with subtle grain and a warm Crew
> Glow light leak from the top-left corner. Centered, a soft frosted-glass card (rounded
> 28px, warm double shadow, faint top inner highlight) holding the recap layout: a top
> zone for a small Tayfa crew-mark and a generic placeholder title; a middle hero zone with
> a tasteful **activity glyph montage** (e.g., a bike + coffee + dice in coherent coral/
> mint duotone, soft long shadows) instead of any photo of real people; a row of three
> abstracted faceless avatar circles (inclusive, no features) implying "your crew"; and a
> lower stats strip with empty rounded chips ready for app-rendered numbers (attendees,
> vibe rating, activity) and a generic fuzzed-area label like "Kadıköy". Mint `#2DD4A7`
> verified tick accent. Geometric-humanist type style for any placeholder words, generous
> spacing, celebratory but tasteful — think Spotify Wrapped meets Apple. Mood: joyful,
> proud, shareable, trustworthy. AVOID: real faces, any address/venue/map/GPS, real names
> or handles, dating/romance framing, cluttered or hard-to-overlay layout, garbled text
> (keep placeholders minimal and generic), neon gaudiness.

## 2. Recap card — square format (1024×1024, opaque)

> Square recap-card variant, 1024×1024, opaque, for in-feed and link previews. Same
> **Dusk Depth** ground and frosted-glass card system, condensed into a balanced square:
> crew-mark top-left, central activity-glyph duotone montage, a row of three abstracted
> faceless avatars, and a single clean stats chip zone left empty for app-rendered numbers
> plus a generic fuzzed-area label. Mint verified accent, warm Crew Glow corner light leak,
> grain. Celebratory, premium, privacy-safe. AVOID: same bans as the vertical card — no
> real faces, no precise location, no real PII, no dating framing, no baked-in data text.
