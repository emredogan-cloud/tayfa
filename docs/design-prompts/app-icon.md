# App Icon & Adaptive Icon

Conventions from `README.md` apply. The icon is the brand's most-reproduced asset — it
must read instantly at 48px on a busy home screen and feel premium at 1024px on the store.

## Concept

A single, confident **"tayfa" mark**: an abstract cluster of three–four rounded forms
leaning together — *people forming a crew* — that also reads as a warm geometric "T".
Friendly, social, distinctly non-corporate. No literal pin, no heart, no chat bubble.

## Master icon prompt (1024×1024, opaque)

> A premium iOS/Android app icon, 1024×1024, opaque. Centered on a smooth **Crew Glow**
> gradient (coral `#FF5A3C` top-left flowing to sunset amber `#FFB23E` bottom-right, 135°,
> subtle film grain to avoid banding). The mark: three to four softly rounded organic
> blobs / pebble shapes overlapping and leaning toward each other like a small crew of
> friends standing close, negative space between them implying a warm geometric letter
> "T". Forms rendered in **Soft Cream `#FFF7F0`** with a faint frosted-glass translucency,
> one form tinted **Mint Verify `#2DD4A7`** as a tiny verified accent dot. Apple-grade
> soft realism: gentle top-left light, a soft inner highlight on the upper edge, large
> warm-tinted ambient shadow beneath the cluster, no hard edges. Modern, geometric,
> joyful, trustworthy. Looks great shrunk to 48px. No text, no letters spelled out, no
> photographic elements. AVOID: flat corporate logo, map pin, heart, flame, chat bubble,
> gloss/bevel, gray shadows, pure white or pure black, dating-app symbolism, busy detail.

## App Store / Play marquee variant (1024×1024, opaque)

Same mark, slightly more depth and a faint Bosphorus-dusk bokeh behind the gradient for
the store listing; mark unchanged so brand recognition holds.

## Adaptive icon — foreground layer (1024×1024, transparent)

> The **mark only** — the rounded crew-cluster forms in Soft Cream with the single Mint
> accent dot and its soft contact shadow — centered on a **transparent** background,
> sized within the Android adaptive safe zone (mark occupies ~62% of canvas, fully inside
> the central circle so no part is clipped by circle/squircle/rounded masks). Crisp,
> self-contained, premium soft-realism rendering. No background fill, no gradient here.

## Adaptive icon — background layer (1024×1024, opaque)

> A full-bleed **Crew Glow** gradient field (`#FF5A3C → #FFB23E`, 135°) with gentle grain
> and a barely-there radial warm glow toward the upper-left, no foreground elements — the
> backdrop layer that parallaxes behind the adaptive foreground mark.

## Monochrome / themed-icon tint layer (1024×1024, transparent)

> The crew-cluster mark as a single flat silhouette in pure white on transparent, even
> stroke weight, no gradient or shadow — for Android 13+ themed icons and notification
> tinting.
