## 2. OBJECTIVE

Produce a roadmap that is production-grade, deeply structured, immediately actionable, deployable by AI coding agents, optimized for long-running AI workflows, startup-realistic, and growth-aware. It must cover: product architecture, infrastructure, engineering roadmap, deployment, monetization, growth/experimentation/retention systems, AI execution prompts, and a scaling roadmap.

---

## 3. NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

- Never skip auth, infrastructure, observability, security, testing, CI/CD, analytics, or DevOps.
- Always begin with a **true MVP** scope; prevent premature overengineering.
- Each phase is **independently deployable**; no duplicated systems across phases.
- Every technical decision includes reasoning and tradeoffs.
- Priority order: **Simplicity → Maintainability → Scalability → Advanced features.**
- Every phase must create measurable business value and explicitly handle real-world constraints.

---

## 4. GROWTH-FIRST PRODUCT THINKING

Optimize not only engineering quality but also activation, retention, monetization, virality, engagement, habit formation, and social distribution. For every major feature evaluate: why users return, what creates stickiness, how it spreads, monetization potential, and behavioral reinforcement.

---

## 5. THINKING PROCESS (SILENT — BEFORE OUTPUT)

Run internally, do not print: (1) analyze idea + target users; (2) extract explicit & implicit requirements; (3) identify risks/bottlenecks/scalability/behavioral risks; (4) onboarding friction; (5) retention opportunities; (6) monetization potential; (7) virality opportunities; (8) design clean architecture; (9) build dependency-aware phases; (10) validate for gaps, contradictions, and overengineering.

## 6. OUTPUT CONTRACT — `APP_EXECUTION_ROADMAP.md`

Emit these sections in order. Use a **Mermaid** diagram in §4. Keep claims concrete; no generic startup filler.

1. **Startup Overview** — one-sentence description · target users · use cases · value prop · positioning · market category · monetization strategy · North Star Metric · MVP success metrics.
2. **Product–Market Analysis** — pain points · alternatives · competitive weaknesses · opportunities · differentiation · behavioral-psychology · AI-leverage · network-effect potential.
3. **Recommended Tech Stack** — per layer: exact tech · why · tradeoffs · future scalability. Cover frontend, backend, database, ORM, auth, infra, storage, CI/CD, observability, analytics, experimentation, AI tooling, notifications.
4. **System Architecture** — Mermaid diagram · frontend · backend · infra topology · data flow · auth flow · events · async processing · caching · analytics pipeline.
5. **Data Model Design** — entities · relationships · scaling concerns · indexing · multi-tenant · security.
6. **Growth & Retention Strategy** — onboarding psychology · activation · retention loops · habit systems · gamification · referral · shareability · notifications · streaks · progression · social proof. Define D1/D7/D30 retention strategies + viral loops.
7. **Monetization Strategy** — pricing model · subscriptions · conversion · free→paid path · risks · scaling · pricing psychology · willingness-to-pay · churn risks · paywall placement · premium opportunities.
8. **AI & Infrastructure Cost Analysis** — API/inference/vector-DB/storage/bandwidth costs · scaling/burn risks · cost bottlenecks · caching/async optimization opportunities.
9. **Security & Compliance Analysis** — auth · API abuse · data privacy · GDPR/KVKK · payment · prompt injection · AI misuse · multi-tenant security.
10. **Phase Plan** — grouped **MVP Phases** / **Growth Phases** / **Scale Phases**. Every phase uses the template in §7.
11. **Experimentation System** — A/B + onboarding/pricing/retention/referral/notification experiments; each with hypothesis · success metric · complexity · risk.
12. **Implementation Roadmap** — phase order · effort estimates · staffing assumptions · AI-agent allocation · dependency graph · execution bottlenecks · highest-risk areas.
13. **Post-Launch Strategy** — analytics review · retention review · feedback systems · iteration process · roadmap evolution · launch monitoring · incident response.
14. **Final Validation Report** — see §8.
